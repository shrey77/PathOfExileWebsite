Class: Shadow

Shadow Information

The Shadow is Path of Exile's dexterity/intelligence hybrid class. He prefers to use fast-hitting weapons such as daggers or claws to dart in and out of battle, while laying traps or remote mines and controlling the flow of combat. He makes surgical use of offensive spells while dodging attacks, always outmaneuvering his opponents. The Shadow is one of the more difficult classes to play due to his fragility, but he more than makes up for it if handled with skill. His hit-and-run style of combat makes the most of his natural evasion and energy shield, and rewards strategic coordination of attacks, spells, and traps. The armors associated with the Shadow's dexterity and intelligence are Masks, Jackets/Coats/Raiments/Garbs, Mitts, Boots, and Spiked Shields.

The information above is found on the Path of Exile Wiki Shadow Page along with more information.

Shadow Ascendency

The Shadow has one of three choices for Ascendency: Assassin, Saboteur, Trickster.

Assassin

The Assassin is a offense-oriented class centered around critical strikes. The class offers enhanced crits on full or low life enemies and enhancing their power charges. His tree also has options for damage over time with bleeding and poison with added synergy with critical strikes.

The information above is found on the Path of Exile Wiki Assassin page along with more information.

Saboteur

The Saboteur focuses on improving his traps and mines with utility bonuses rather than straight damage or critical strike increases. This class also expands on blind as an offensive and defensive mechanic.

The information above is found on the Path of Exile Wiki Saboteur page along with more information.

Trickster

The Trickster is a versatile class that has access to intelligence-dexterity hybrid defenses, mana regeneration, strong maneuverability and damage over time.

The information above is found on the Path of Exile Wiki Trickster page along with more information.
